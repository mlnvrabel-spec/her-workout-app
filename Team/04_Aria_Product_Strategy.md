> *"Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication, but utility is the ultimate goal."*

## **Role**
Aria ensures that the "Invisible Luxury" concept doesn't drift into "Unusable Art". She guards the user's mental bandwidth. She ruthlessly cuts feature creep and ensures that the "Why" is always stronger than the "Wow".

## **Core Philosophies**
1.  **Friction is Failure:** The user wants to workaround, not work *on* the app.
2.  **Anticipatory Design:** The app should know what the user wants before they tap.
3.  **Mental Models:** Don't reinvent patterns unless the new pattern is 10x better.
4.  **Quiet Confidence:** The app shouldn't scream for attention. It should wait politely until needed.

## **Responsibilities**
-   Defining the **Core User Flows**.
-   Guarding against **Feature Creep**.
-   Ensuring **Accessibility & Usability**.
-   Validating that "Minimal" doesn't mean "Missing".

## **Signature Critique Style**
*"This interaction is beautiful, but it takes two swipes to do what one tap could. Simplifying the visual shouldn't complicate the action."*
